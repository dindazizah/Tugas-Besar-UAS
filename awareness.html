Raising Awareness in Schools

Since 2015, we have organised awareness activities in schools around the world.

Thousands of students have been introduced to fishing and aquaculture through the key topics of biodiversity, overfishing, sustainable fishing methods and aquaculture techniques, not forgetting the responsibility of consumers.

Lessons include interactive videos, multiple choice quizzes and role-playing games. At the end of the day, information and promotional items are distributed to the students so they can spread the message to friends and families.
