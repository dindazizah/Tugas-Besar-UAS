Protecting coastal habitats in Belize through ambitious climate commitments

The Belize Government, in collaboration with us, The Pew Charitable Trusts, University of Belize's Environmental Research Institute and other partners, is aiming to strengthen protections of the country's coastal ecosystems.

Helping People Fish Better

Since unsustainable fishing is a problem entirely caused by human behavior, people have the power to fix it.

Plastics

We are fighting for a world with no plastic in nature by 2030.

Umah Lumba Rehabilitation, Release, and Retirement Center

The Umah Lumba Center is a purpose-built facility for recently confiscated dolphins from captive facilities, and for stranded or injured dolphins.

Dolphin Project: A Voice For All Dolphins

While the COVID-19 pandemic continues to rage throughout the world, Dolphin Project continues to maintain a global presence, acting as a voice on behalf of all dolphins in need.

Raising Awareness in School

Organizing awareness activities in schools around the world.
